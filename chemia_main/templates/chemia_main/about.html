Chemia handmade products - started in 2012, in the corner of Nika's dining table. Then it was just an experiment to see if Nika could make a simple belt. Instead of doing one, he did six or seven - each repetition getting better and better. Soon others were asking for Nika's things: belts, wallets and other crafts.  We often say: "It was a curiosity that turned into a hobby, turned into a business, and became a brand."

Although our activities are focused on creating different and individual products for customers, we also produce corporate orders for a growing list of partners and customers. Our product philosophy is to keep things simple. Our designs hint at classic style and focus on pragmatism. We want our customers to be confident that Chemia belts, wallets and bags are durable enough to travel the world with them, while being suitable for everyday use. Our products are made in Georgia.
